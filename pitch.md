# ClinicFlow Video Pitch Script & Shot List

**Duration**: 60–90 seconds

---

## 🎬 Shot List & Narration

### Shot 1: The Problem (0:00 - 0:15)
* **Visual**: Camera pans over a busy desk or phone showing missed calls.
* **Script**: *"Scheduling a doctor's appointment shouldn't require waiting on hold or navigating complex, slow patient portals. ClinicFlow brings the clinic directly into the chat workspace where you already spend your day."*

### Shot 2: The Trigger (0:15 - 0:35)
* **Visual**: Screen capture of Slack. The user DMs the bot: *"I have bad flu symptoms and need a doctor."*
* **Script**: *"By simply direct messaging the ClinicFlow agent, the patient triggers our interactive triage card. The bot parses the message and queries our database for available specialists."*

### Shot 3: The "Aha!" Moment (0:35 - 1:00)
* **Visual**: Show the bot listing available doctors with `Book` buttons. User clicks `Book`. The screen shifts to show a database update in Supabase, followed by showing the confirmation email received in an inbox.
* **Script**: *"Aha! With a single click, our agent books the appointment in Supabase and triggers an automated notification email via Resend. The transaction is complete in under 5 seconds."*

### Shot 4: The Close (1:00 - 1:15)
* **Visual**: Logo of ClinicFlow with links.
* **Script**: *"ClinicFlow removes administrative friction, making healthcare booking as simple as sending a message. Try it out on our live demo. Thank you."*
